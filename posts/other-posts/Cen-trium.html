New competition - Cen-trium

Nov 17th 2023 4 Min read

Cen-trium has just recently entered the game, now being live for 12 days at the time this article is being written. They currently have made over 159K+ turnover since their launch, with this platform showing a lot of strong potential in the coming months. They currently have 3 active packages available which can be activated by making your first deposit, they range from 50 USD / 500 USD and 5000 USD equivalent.

These packages vary in earning potential; so the basic entry package can generate 2.20% profit per day over an accrual period of 15 days, with the accrual terms - Mon - Fri with the capital returned at the end of the plan. This package is great if you’re someone new to crypto and your risk tolerance is lower than most. It is also affordable and can be a great start for the everyday investor.

The next package is the Optiamal package which is $500 USD equivalent, with the profit percentage being 2.40% per day, the accrual period is over 25 business days, Accrual terms - Mon - Fri, with the capital returned at the end of the plan. This would be more of an intermediate investment which is for someone who has a slightly higher tolerance to risk and is prepared to chuck a larger stake into gaining larger returns. You also have the option to take the original capital out at the end of the accrual period or reinvest the funds into the account to further the growth of the investment portfolio.

The next one is the premium package which sits at 5000 USD equivalent, this currently is their largest package and requires the highest risk tolerance depending on your disposable income and financial experience or situation. This also has a higher profit percentage which is 2.80% per day, which has an accrual period of 35 business days, the accrual terms are - Mon - Fri, while the capital gets returned at the end of the plan. Out of the three packages, the overall return will be significantly higher than the other two packages mentioned earlier, however, someone has the capacity to work up to this by entering the game at an entry level and over time as their portfolio grows they can gradually work their way up in steps to larger packages with their original portfolio if they allow compounding to do its work over time, you’ll get the results you want in no time if you’re serious about changing your financial situation.

I would take your time to research each company you decide to put your crypto into, there’s various ways you can assess how good of a fit the company is for your investment and whether you want to allocate some of your funds into their platform to gain the returns you want.

You can join the platform at https://cen-trium.com/?ref=victorpb

You reach a point in life where an outcome approach begins to outweigh the benefits of working for an hourly wage as there are only 24 hours in a day, as your results increase so can your earnings at an exponential rate.
